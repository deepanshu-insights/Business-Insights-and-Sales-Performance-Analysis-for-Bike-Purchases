# Business-Insights-and-Sales-Performance-Analysis-for-Bike-Purchases
Examined 1,000 customer records to uncover bike-buying patterns across key demographic variables including age, income, occupation, and commute distance.
Applied age brackets, distance ranges, and job category groupings to organize data for clearer comparison and trend discovery.
Built pivot tables and interactive charts in Excel to visualize purchasing trends across regions, gender, marital status, and education backgrounds.
Found that middle-aged working professionals and nearby shoppers (within 1 mile) drove the largest share of sales, revealing clear potential for focused marketing campaigns.
